Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 2024

Last Updated: December 2024

Introduction

AI Podcast Summarizer is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Audio Files: Podcast episodes you upload for processing
  • URLs: Podcast URLs you provide for analysis

Information We Don't Collect

  • Personal Information: No names, emails, or contact information
  • Account Data: No user accounts or profiles
  • Location Data: No device location access
  • Device Information: No device identifiers collected

How We Use Your Information

Audio files and URLs are processed temporarily to generate transcripts and summaries using Google's Gemini AI service. All processing is performed solely to provide app functionality.

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Results are stored locally on your device
  • No Server Storage: We don't store your data on our servers
  • Data Deletion: Uninstalling the app removes all data

Third-Party Services

We use Google Gemini AI for text analysis. Audio transcripts are sent to Gemini AI for processing under their privacy policy.

Your Rights

  • Data Control: All data is stored locally on your device
  • Deletion: Uninstall the app to remove all data
  • No Tracking: We don't track or profile users

Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Contact Information

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us via GitHub issues on our repository.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page.

By using AI Podcast Summarizer, you agree to this Privacy Policy.
